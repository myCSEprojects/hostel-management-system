About Us

Welcome to the Hostel Management System of IIT Gandhinagar. A centralized, accurate, and up-to-date source of information for the hostel. If you are looking for data related to hostel residents, hostel staff and details, hostel equipment, hostel utilities, room history of a resident then you’re in the right place. We are here to provide a comprehensive and intuitive service to help you manage your hostel easily and conveniently. We offer a wide range of features and services such as booking, availability and check-in/out management, online payment services, customer relation management and more. Our goal is to make hostel management as easy and stress-free as possible. With our user-friendly platform, you can save time and energy, focus on improving your business and serve your customers better. Thank you for trusting us with your needs. Plus, you can keep up with the latest news, updates, and events in the hostel community. Thanks for choosing us and we look forward to making your hostel experience the best it can be.
